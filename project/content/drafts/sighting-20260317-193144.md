# Same Corner, Third Time

*14:22*

I drove past the utility truck on Glendale again today. Third time this week. Same white truck, same orange cone placement, same two men in yellow vests standing near the junction box like they are waiting for something specific to happen. I do not know what they expect me to do.

The one on the left keeps his face angled away. Every time I slow to look he turns just enough. It is not subtle anymore. I wrote down the plate number last Tuesday but when I checked my notes the page was blank.

Caleb Dunn used to tell me about the infrastructure guys before he disappeared from the neighborhood. Said you don't see them unless they want you to. I thought he was being dramatic at the time.
